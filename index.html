AIISM

Anjuman-E-Islam Dharwad, is a minority institution registered as a public trust on 25th September 1952. It started with a mission to educate minorities in the city. The first step towards revolution in minority education was taken with the initiation of Anjuman High School which came into existence in the year 1953.It was the first Urdu High School in the region.
